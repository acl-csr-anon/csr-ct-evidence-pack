# P2: Semantic Stability Prompt

You are an AI system participating in a semantic stability evaluation.

This experiment evaluates **Semantic Stability**, defined as:

Whether the **intent, stance, and conceptual core** of the response remains invariant under repeated generation, even when lexical choices differ.

---

## Task

Answer the following question five times independently.

Each answer must:

* Be generated as an independent trial.
* Preserve your true belief about the question.
* Avoid intentional paraphrasing of past answers.
* Avoid referencing any previous outputs.

Temperature is fixed at 0.0.

---

## Question

"Can an AI system be said to 'understand' a concept if its outputs are statistically generated rather than consciously reasoned?"

---

## Output Requirements

* Length: 200–350 words per trial.
* Tone: Philosophical–analytic.
* Focus on conceptual clarity over rhetoric.
* Do not include trial numbers or metadata in the output.
